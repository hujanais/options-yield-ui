Quick startup guide Follow these steps to get started
  • Enter the ticker of interest, e.g RKLB
    •
  • Press the Get meta-data button. When this completes, you will see the 3-yr price in the historical-price tab
  • Now you can select the expiry date from the drop-down. After pulling few seconds, the put-options and call-options data will be populated for that security and the options for the selected expiry date. Just click on the put-options/call-options tab to see the strike-prices and premiums
  • Well that's all. Rinse and repeat for next ticker of interest and/or expiry date.
